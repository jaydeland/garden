# 🪨 Sedum Spirit

**Category:** Foliage & Filler
**Source:** OSC

## Regency Character

She blooms when her contemporaries have long retired. A perennial of admirable persistence.

## Social Media Angle

Flat pink heads when everything else has faded — 'September's most underrated plant'

## Growing Notes

- **Start Indoors:** —
- **Direct Sow:** Apr–May
- **Bloom Period:** Aug–Oct
- **Height:** 35cm
- **Primary Use:** Perennial succulent, pollinator magnet

## Planting Details

direct sow Apr–May at the very front of this strip; a hardy Zone 6b perennial — mark clearly so it is not accidentally pulled before establishing in Year 1; blooms Aug–Oct in flat terracotta-pink heads when most other plants have faded.
