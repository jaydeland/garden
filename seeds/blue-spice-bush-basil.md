# 🫐 Blue Spice Bush Basil

**Category:** Kitchen Garden
**Source:** OSC

## Regency Character

Of unusual colouring and surprising fragrance — simultaneously ornamental and edible. Overqualified for the salad.

## Social Media Angle

Blue-tinged basil nobody has heard of — 'this exists?!' content

## Growing Notes

- **Start Indoors:** Apr
- **Direct Sow:** late May
- **Bloom Period:** Harvest Jun–frost
- **Height:** 35cm
- **Primary Use:** Ornamental basil, vanilla-anise scent

## Planting Details

start indoors Apr–May, transplant late May only after soil is consistently warm above 15\u00b0C — basil collapses below 10\u00b0C; space 25–30cm apart.
