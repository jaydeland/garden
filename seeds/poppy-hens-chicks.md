# 🐣 Poppy Hens & Chicks

**Category:** Cut Flowers
**Source:** Stems

## Regency Character

Eccentric in form and utterly charming. The most talked-about arrival of the Season.

## Social Media Angle

Bizarre crown-shaped pods = viral potential

## Growing Notes

- **Start Indoors:** —
- **Direct Sow:** early Apr
- **Bloom Period:** Jun–Jul
- **Height:** 75cm
- **Primary Use:** Cut flower, novelty pod

## Planting Details

direct sow onto cold soil in early April — scatter along the front edge, press lightly, do NOT cover (light required for germination). Once seedlings reach 5cm, thin to 20–25cm spacing. The bizarre crown-shaped pods are the season's most photographed detail — leave pods standing after petals drop. Zone 6b: sow as soon as soil can be worked, even if frost is still possible.
