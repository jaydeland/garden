# 💜 Lavender

**Category:** Foliage & Filler
**Source:** OSC

## Regency Character

Of all the Continent's offerings, lavender alone perfumes the air with equal parts calm and ambition.

## Social Media Angle

Lavender bundles = instant lifestyle content — sachets, bees, dried arrangements

## Growing Notes

- **Start Indoors:** Feb–Mar
- **Direct Sow:** Apr–May
- **Bloom Period:** Jul–Aug (Y2)
- **Height:** 45cm
- **Primary Use:** Perennial herb, dried, pollinator

## Planting Details

start indoors Feb–Mar, 8+ weeks with gentle bottom heat; transplant after last frost in May, 45cm apart in the sunniest, best-drained position — perennial in Zone 6b with sharp drainage; modest bloom Year 1, full flowering Year 2.
