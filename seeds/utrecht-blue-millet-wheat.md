# 🌾 Utrecht Blue Millet & Wheat

**Category:** Foliage & Filler
**Source:** Stems

## Regency Character

Neither flower nor fruit, yet indispensable to the composition. Quietly magnificent.

## Social Media Angle

Blue-toned grains = elevated autumn/dried arrangements

## Growing Notes

- **Start Indoors:** Apr
- **Direct Sow:** May
- **Bloom Period:** Aug–frost
- **Height:** 90cm
- **Primary Use:** Dried flower, texture

## Planting Details

direct sow May after last frost, 15cm spacing in a back row — harvest seed heads when fully blue-grey (Aug–Sep) for dried arrangements; leave a few stems standing for birds over winter.
