# 💎 Nigella Miss Jekyll

**Category:** Cut Flowers
**Source:** Order 1

## Regency Character

A bloom of quiet refinement, much admired by those of discerning taste.

## Social Media Angle

Ethereal blue blooms + alien seed pods — content goldmine

## Growing Notes

- **Start Indoors:** Apr
- **Direct Sow:** May
- **Bloom Period:** Jul–Sep
- **Height:** 45cm
- **Primary Use:** Cut flower, filler

## Planting Details

direct sow ALL FOUR IN PLACE in Apr — do not transplant; scatter thickly, barely cover, thin to 15cm when 5cm tall. Interplant the four varieties in drifts for a layered tonal effect. Ozark Sundrop (45cm): direct sow Apr–May in this same band; a Zone 6b perennial that opens evening-through-morning — mark clearly so it is not disturbed; will strengthen in Year 2.
