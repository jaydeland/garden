# 🏔️ Edelweiss

**Category:** Cut Flowers
**Source:** OSC

## Regency Character

She descended from the Austrian Alps to grace a Norfolk County garden. We are honoured. She is indifferent.

## Social Media Angle

'I grew Edelweiss in Ontario' — the audacity alone is content

## Growing Notes

- **Start Indoors:** Feb–Mar
- **Direct Sow:** Apr
- **Bloom Period:** Jun–Aug
- **Height:** 22cm
- **Primary Use:** Alpine novelty, dried, edging

## Planting Details

start indoors Feb–Mar, surface-sow (needs light to germinate), transplant May in a clump at the front centre — alpine novelty; keep on the drier side.
