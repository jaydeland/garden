# 🩰 Shirley Poppy Pastel Doubles

**Category:** Cut Flowers
**Source:** Stems

## Regency Character

Translucent, trembling, and heartbreakingly brief. The fleeting beauty of youth itself.

## Social Media Angle

Crêpe-paper petals in backlight = guaranteed saves

## Growing Notes

- **Start Indoors:** —
- **Direct Sow:** early Apr
- **Bloom Period:** Jun–Jul
- **Height:** 60cm
- **Primary Use:** Tissue-paper blooms

## Planting Details

direct sow cold soil early April — scatter freely through the middle third of the meadow, barely press in, do not cover. Interplant with Red Breadseed for a layered bloom effect. Thin to 15cm once seedlings emerge. Harvest as buds for longest vase life; petals are translucent at golden hour — photograph backlit in evening light.
