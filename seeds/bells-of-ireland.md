# 🍀 Bells of Ireland

**Category:** Cut Flowers
**Source:** OSC

## Regency Character

She provides no colour of her own — and yet every arrangement improves dramatically in her presence.

## Social Media Angle

All-green stems = the florist's secret weapon for making colour pop

## Growing Notes

- **Start Indoors:** Mar–Apr
- **Direct Sow:** Apr
- **Bloom Period:** Jul–Sep
- **Height:** 75cm
- **Primary Use:** Architectural filler, fresh + dried

## Planting Details

cold-stratify seed 2 weeks in the fridge before starting indoors Mar–Apr, or direct sow Apr; space 30cm apart — architectural filler used both fresh and dried; cut before calyxes brown for longest vase life.
