# ✨ Zinnia Zinderella Lilac

**Category:** Cut Flowers
**Source:** Order 1

## Regency Character

She arrived at the garden party and nobody could identify her. Deliciously scandalous.

## Social Media Angle

Unusual bloom form — 'Is this even a zinnia?' hook

## Growing Notes

- **Start Indoors:** Apr
- **Direct Sow:** late May
- **Bloom Period:** Jul–frost
- **Height:** 60cm
- **Primary Use:** Scabiosa-type cut

## Planting Details

start indoors Apr, transplant after last frost, space 25–30cm apart. These three form the main cutting block — pinch hard at 30cm for maximum branching. Succession-sow every 3 weeks through May for harvest extending into frost. Molotov's cactus-spined petals contrast with Zinderella's scabiosa form and Peaches + Cream's soft doubles — mix in arrangements freely.
