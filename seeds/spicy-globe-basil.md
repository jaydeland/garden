# 🫧 Spicy Globe Basil

**Category:** Kitchen Garden
**Source:** OSC

## Regency Character

Small, perfectly spherical, and rather formidably flavoured. The terrier of the herb border.

## Social Media Angle

Perfect dome shape = the most photogenic herb in the garden

## Growing Notes

- **Start Indoors:** Apr–May
- **Direct Sow:** late May
- **Bloom Period:** Harvest Jun–frost
- **Height:** 25cm
- **Primary Use:** Compact ornamental basil, spicy

## Planting Details

start indoors Apr–May, transplant late May only after soil is consistently warm above 15\u00b0C — basil collapses below 10\u00b0C; space 25–30cm apart. Brussels Sprouts Flower Sprout (60cm): start indoors Apr, transplant May, 45cm spacing at the end of the herb row — harvest Oct after first frost for best flavour.
