# 🟤 Brown Sugar Tomato

**Category:** Kitchen Garden
**Source:** Moonglow

## Regency Character

Dark, complex, and possessed of uncommon depth. One does not simply slice him on toast.

## Social Media Angle

Moody brown tomato = 'not your ordinary garden' content

## Growing Notes

- **Start Indoors:** Mar
- **Direct Sow:** —
- **Bloom Period:** Harvest Aug–Sep
- **Height:** 150cm
- **Primary Use:** Heirloom, rich flavour

## Planting Details

tall indeterminate — use 5-foot cages, tie in every 20cm. Wolverine Tomato (120cm): semi-indeterminate, cage. Moonglow Jewel Tomato F2 (varies): F2 genetic mix — expect size variation; mark each plant individually. Candyland Tomato (90cm): produces cascading clusters of 20+ tiny fruits — give a strong cage and 75cm spacing for air circulation. Ground Cherry (60cm): plant at the end of the row; husk fruit drops when ripe — harvest from the ground daily.
