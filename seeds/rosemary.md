# 🌿 Rosemary

**Category:** Kitchen Garden
**Source:** OSC

## Regency Character

For remembrance — and for lamb. No estate garden is complete without her.

## Social Media Angle

Rosemary in bouquets — 'yes, it belongs in arrangements too'

## Growing Notes

- **Start Indoors:** Feb–Mar
- **Direct Sow:** —
- **Bloom Period:** Foliage + blue flowers Jun
- **Height:** 60cm
- **Primary Use:** Culinary herb, aromatic, bee magnet

## Planting Details

start indoors Feb–Mar (slow: 3–4 weeks germination), transplant May after last frost, 45cm spacing in the sunniest position — not fully hardy in Zone 6b; bring indoors before Oct frost or treat as an annual.
