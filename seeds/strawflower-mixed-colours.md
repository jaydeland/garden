# 🏵️ Strawflower Mixed Colours

**Category:** Cut Flowers
**Source:** OSC

## Regency Character

Her petals have the texture of fine paper and the longevity of a well-placed rumour.

## Social Media Angle

Crispy petals that hold colour for years — the zero-waste flower

## Growing Notes

- **Start Indoors:** Apr
- **Direct Sow:** May
- **Bloom Period:** Jul–frost
- **Height:** 75cm
- **Primary Use:** Dried flower, everlasting

## Planting Details

start indoors Apr (do not cover — needs light to germinate), transplant May, 25cm spacing — harvest for drying when outer petals open but centre still closed.
