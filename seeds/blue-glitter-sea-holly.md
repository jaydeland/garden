# 🌊 Blue Glitter Sea Holly

**Category:** Cut Flowers
**Source:** Moonglow

## Regency Character

Spined, steely, and magnificently architectural. She does not ask to be held gently.

## Social Media Angle

Metallic blue = stunning in dried arrangements year-round

## Growing Notes

- **Start Indoors:** Feb–Mar
- **Direct Sow:** Apr
- **Bloom Period:** Jul–Sep
- **Height:** 70cm
- **Primary Use:** Dried flower, textural

## Planting Details

start indoors Feb–Mar (cold stratify 4 weeks), transplant May, 30cm spacing — dislikes wet roots; ensure excellent drainage; harvest metallic-blue bracts when fully coloured for fresh or dried use.
