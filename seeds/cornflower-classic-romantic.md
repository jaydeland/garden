# 🌸 Cornflower Classic Romantic

**Category:** Cut Flowers
**Source:** Order 1

## Regency Character

The very picture of a country romance. Penelope Featherington energy.

## Social Media Angle

Cottage garden aesthetic — trending on garden TikTok

## Growing Notes

- **Start Indoors:** Apr
- **Direct Sow:** May
- **Bloom Period:** Jun–Aug
- **Height:** 80cm
- **Primary Use:** Cottage cut flower

## Planting Details

start indoors Apr or direct sow May, 15cm spacing — may sprawl; insert thin bamboo canes at planting time before they need them. African Marigold Glow Up Gold (90cm): start indoors Mar–Apr, transplant May, 45cm spacing — tallest marigold available; stake with a robust cane in the back row. Blue Glitter Sea Holly (70cm): start indoors Feb–Mar (cold stratify 4 weeks), transplant May, 30cm spacing — dislikes wet roots; ensure excellent drainage; harvest metallic-blue bracts when fully coloured for fresh or dried use. Snapdragon Rocket Hybrids (100cm) and Snapdragon Panorama Mixed (75cm): start indoors Mar–Apr, transplant May, 25cm spacing — tallest plants in this bed; insert canes at planting. Lupins Russells Red (100cm): start indoors Mar, transplant May, 45cm spacing — perennial, mark permanently, do NOT pull in autumn; blooms Jun–Jul Year 2.
